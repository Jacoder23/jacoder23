A Quick Mini-Article About My Speed Reading Techinque

I did a test and I got 889 WPM with 79% Comprehension.

I never actually speed read, I just break things down into simplified events and then later merge the event together.

This is really fast but actually lowers your comprehension significantly since instead of remembering "Fido, his dog died yesterday." I remember "His dog died yesterday."

I mostly use it for times when I forgot to study for a quiz. :)
